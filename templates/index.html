Why Cache?

For users who value complete control over their knowledge. Powerful personal knowledge management with no vendor lock-in, no privacy compromises—just fast, portable notes synced through git.

📷 Privacy demo

Your Notes, Your Data

You are in total control of your notes and privacy. Cache has zero backend - everything stays on your devices, synced through your own git server. Complete privacy. No tracking. No exceptions.

📷 Markdown demo

Beautiful Markdown

Your notes are stored, viewed and edited as clean portable markdown. No proprietary formats, no lock-in. Your knowledge remains accessible forever in files you own, readable in any text editor.

📷 Git sync demo

Git Sync

Cache is a Git client - use GitHub, GitLab, Gitea, your own server, or any Git service. Version control built-in means you never lose your work and you control who has access.

📷 Open source demo

Open Ecosystem

We've open-sourced cache_core—the engine that manages notes and search. Build your own compatible apps, audit the code, or contribute improvements.

📷 Speed demo

Lightning Fast

Built around speed to get ideas out of your head quickly. Fluid touch gestures or comprehensive keyboard shortcuts, minimal interface, instant search - nothing slows you down on any device.

📷 Linking demo

Powerful Linking

Build a connected knowledge base your way. Add explicit links or define smart patterns that connect notes automatically—without cluttering your source files.

📷 Search demo

Blazing Fast Search

Find anything instantly across your entire knowledge base. Line-level results show context immediately—no waiting, no indexing delays, just results.

📷 Journal demo

First-Class Journaling

Date-based notes with first-class support for effortless journaling. Browse, search, and edit your daily entries seamlessly. Journals and reference notes coexist with fully configurable categories.

📷 Modal editing demo

Modal Editing

Optionally use powerful modal editing with Vim and Helix motions. For power users who want maximum efficiency, or stick with simple mode for traditional editing.

📷 Offline demo

Offline First

Carry your notes with you. Your devices always have your full notes repository. Work anywhere, anytime, without depending on internet connectivity.

📷 Platform demo

Cross-Platform

Built for every platform - open-source Linux and privacy-focused GrapheneOS, plus macOS, Windows, iOS, and Android. Optimized for any screen size. One powerful experience, everywhere.

📷 Simplicity demo

Opinionated Minimalism

Every feature serves one purpose: managing your markdown notes faster and better. No plugins means no bloat, no distractions, no performance compromises. Just powerful note-taking, nothing more.
