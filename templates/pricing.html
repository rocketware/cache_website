FAQ

Why isn't Cache free?

Cache is independently built with a focus on privacy — no data harvesting, no data brokers, no advertisements. Zero information is collected about you or your notes. Your subscription directly funds continued development and improvements to the app.

Are payments cross-platform?

Yes. Your license is stored in your notes repository and synced via git. Upgrade once, and all your devices receive it automatically. If you change git providers, Cache will reassign your license. No accounts required.

What information is captured during payment?

Cache only stores a hash of your repository's git remote URL to verify your subscription. (This hash is irreversible, it identifies your subscription without revealing any information.) No other identifiable information is captured by us. The payment provider (Stripe, Apple App Store, Google Play) will collect the information required to process your payment according to their privacy policies. For more information see the Privacy Policy.

Do I lose my notes if I cancel?

Never. Your notes live in your own Git repository as plain markdown files — you can take them to any app. Cache will continue to work — reading, searching, tasks — but if you have more than 50 notes, you won't be able to create new ones.
